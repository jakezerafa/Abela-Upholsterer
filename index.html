Our Story

A Family Tradition
in Malta Since 2009

Based in Msida, Malta, Abela Upholsterer has been the trusted name for quality upholstery work for over 15 years. We are a family-run workshop dedicated to the craft of upholstery — treating every piece as if it were our own.

Whether you bring us a cherished heirloom sofa, a car with worn seats, or leather that needs restoration, we approach every job with the same care, precision, and passion for quality.
