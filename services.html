Our Services

At Abela Upholsterer we work on various projects related to automotive upholstery. In our years of experience we have managed to work on all types of vehicles — from cars and vans to luxury vehicles, aircraft, and leather restoration. We also offer windscreen replacement and many more services.

Service 01

Automobile Upholstery

From seat repairs to full custom interior makeovers — we work with leather, alcantara, and premium fabrics.

  • Car Seats — Repair & Full Recover
  • Door Panels & Trim
  • Headliners & Roof Linings
  • Convertible Hoods & Soft Tops
  • Dashboard & Steering Wheel Wrap
